The Science Behind the Serenity.

Seeing the Invisible

XCare utilizes advanced 60 GHz millimeter-wave technology. Unlike a camera, it perceives the space using safe, low-power radio waves. It can accurately detect the most subtle vital signs, like the rise and fall of a chest or a heartbeat, even through blankets or in complete darkness.

On-Device Intelligence

Your family's privacy is paramount. That's why all sensitive data is analyzed locally on XCare's dedicated AI processor. Alerts are only sent when an emergency is detected. No images are ever captured, and no raw health data is stored in the cloud.
